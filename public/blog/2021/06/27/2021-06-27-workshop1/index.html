Workshop coming soon...

By Essia Hamouda | June 27, 2021

We are excited for the kickoff of our workshop series. Our first and upcoming workshop will be on January 13th - 14th, 2022 and hope you are too. A few notes:

  • Make sure you have the necessary software installed on your laptop: R, R Studio, git, and Nano. There are setup instructions on the workshop website. If you have any trouble, send us an email or come a little early on Monday. Someone will be there by 8:30 (Mon) or 8:45 (Tue-Thu) to help with installation issues and answer general questions.
